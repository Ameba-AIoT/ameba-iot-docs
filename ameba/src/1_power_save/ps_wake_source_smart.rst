   A hardware SYSON power management control module (SYSON PMC) is designed to control the clock and power of LP,
   and then LP controls the clock and power of NP and AP. When the system enters sleep mode,
   CPUs can select to enter clock-gating (CG) or power-gating (PG) mode,
   while SYSON PMC maintained active to wake up LP when wakeup sources are triggered.
